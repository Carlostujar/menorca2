Presentation editors

Tiago Reurer, Graphic designer, Photographer and editor of this publication.

More than a book, this publication presents an authentic profile of the island. It doesn’t have the privilege of being the first of its style, but it is the first time that over 8000 copies are being printed to be distributed between the principal hotels so that the visitor has all the information at their fingertips. The contents offer an ample vision of Menorca, describing its prehistory, history, primary sectors, products, fiestas, towns… It also contains further interesting information: Cami de Cavalls, calendar of fiestas, fairs and markets, restaurants, etc. and if that is not sufficient, the book finishes with an attractive and innovative idea, an application to download the book in an interactive digital format in 7 languages! It can then be consulted in an easy and comfortable way.

Enjoy your stay. Enjoy Menorca “100 x 100”.



Benet Guardia, Gallery owner, Commercial Director and editor of this publication.

Menorca is one of the most beautiful islands in existence. Though small it includes fascinating and surprising treasures; from an impressive prehistory to the historical footprints of diverse civilizations; plus spectacular countryside. Add to this a temperate climate, friendly people, excellent food, handicrafts, industry, culture and a wide offer of activities. Discover its charm and absorb the peace, security and calm.

In this book we are offering the vision and knowledge of a select team of collaborators so that you can learn more about our origins, history and present reality. You will also find numerous suggestions so that you can enjoy your holiday to the full. Finally, trust in our hand-picked advertisers who will do their best to offer you superb service.

Welcome and enjoy Menorca.
