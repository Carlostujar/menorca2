Menorca 100 x 100

No-one is left indifferent to Menorca

Salomé Cabrera, Councillor for Tourism

No-one is left indifferent to Menorca as the island has the potential to satisfy visitor’s expectations. Menorca 100 x 100 encompasses in its pages a selection of high quality possibilities from the wide range available.

British Menorca, the placid character of the people, peaceful unspoilt areas, on the one hand and historic city centres, active municipalities with initiatives on the other: the multiple opportunities to practice outdoor sports thanks to the privileged climate make the island a place to put in the diary as an almost obligatory visit at any time of the year.

From the Department of Tourism in the Consell Insular we believe that this type of publication can help our island to be better known, and contribute to making your stay in Menorca as enjoyable as possible. Welcome and we hope you have a happy time here with us.
