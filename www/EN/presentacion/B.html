Welcome to Menorca

Santiago Tadeo, President of the Island’s Council (Consell Insular de Menorca)

Welcome to Menorca, Biosphere Reserve, a unique island in the world. Our heritage is a vast inheritance from different civilizations; Megalithic, Roman, Vandal, Byzantine, Muslim, the mediaeval Christian conquest in 1287 up to the present times, the island has also passed through British and French hands and they have left their imprint in our language, buildings and traditions.

On these pages you will find examples of the legacy left by conquerors and settlers that has made our territory so singular and diverse. Menorca 100 x 100 gives an in depth study of the richness and balance of the island, the artisans, the costume jewellery, the leather industry, local products and farming, sectors of great importance in developing the economy.

I invite you to enjoy our beaches, our beauty spots, our tranquillity, our gastronomy, our horses, our fiestas (festivals) and our culture. Finally I invite you to live a great experience and savour every one of the hidden marvels of our land.
