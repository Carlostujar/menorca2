Menorca, open and friendly

Joan Melis, President of ASHOME and Vice president of CAEB

Menorca offers itself to the visitor in an open, friendly way and as hoteliers we are delighted to help make the island a perfect holiday destination. The respect for the environment and tranquillity that reigns on the island make it a perfect place to disconnect and find peace of mind. The offer for the visitor is varied, hotels, apart hotels, apartment complexes, rural hotels, boutique hotels, agricultural tourism, hostels and holiday homes.

The hotel offer is modern and of high quality as hotels have adapted to new demands from visitors. Since the beginning of 1995 hoteliers have developed different initiatives directed towards a philosophy of quality tourism that cares for the environment.

Quality as an instrument to greater achievement has been adopted by many hotel businesses, to consolidate the position of the island as a leader in the sector and to differentiate Menorca as a tourist destination.

Our thanks to you for sharing some days with us; we hope you have a happy holiday. Welcome to Menorca.
