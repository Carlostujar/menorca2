Menorca 100 x 100

Presentation editors

Tiago Reurer, Graphic designer, Photographer and editor of this publication.

More than a book, this publication presents an authentic profile of the island. It doesn’t have the privilege of being the first of its style, but it is the first time that over 8000 copies are being printed to be distributed between the principal hotels so that the visitor has all the information at their fingertips. The contents offer an ample vision of Menorca, describing its prehistory, history, primary sectors, products, fiestas, towns… It also contains further interesting information: Cami de Cavalls, calendar of fiestas, fairs and markets, restaurants, etc. and if that is not sufficient, the book finishes with an attractive and innovative idea, an application to download the book in an interactive digital format in 7 languages! It can then be consulted in an easy and comfortable way. Enjoy your stay. Enjoy Menorca “100 x 100”.
