Art

Menorca has its own special luminosity that seduces and makes all who visit fall in love with it. It is therefore unsurprising that this is the land of artists, both local and foreign who have succumbed to its enchantment.

The whole island is a real art factory, good art. Here you can find works in all styles, formats, prices…

Menorca Art Gallery is the first gallery 100 x 100 on line in the Balearic Islands, it is dedicated to promote and export art from the island to the rest of the world.

It offers wide ranging carefully selected works on a complete web. This solid project is to promote and value the prodigious production here, exporting it worldwide.

If you can, take a painting with you. Or better still order one. You will receive it at home, comfortably, without risk and a total guarantee. It will be a wonderful reminder of your stay on the island, elegant and stylish.

Enjoy Menorca, enjoy its art.
